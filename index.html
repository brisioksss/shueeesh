What are nodes?

A "ROLAND" node is the latter concoction. A node-token project that uses the concept and function of a node to perpetuate a token reward mechanism and keep users engaged. Of course, ROLAND recognizes that the process of "cycling" that must occur to keep the reward distribution both profitable and stable is reliant upon volume over time.

When will the Presale go live?

The sale is planned to launch at ( 9th of February at 9PM UTC Time )
The sale ends 24hours after at ( 10th of February at 9PM UTC Time )

How can I participate in the Public sale?

In order to Participate in the Public Sale you have to get Whitelisted, join us on Discord and sucessfully finish one of the task as described in channel #tasks to get on the whitelist.

How do I benefit from the Public sale?

Once Presale is launched, you can purchase Tokens with $MIM (Magic Internet Money)
on the Avalanche (AVAX) blockchain.
During the sale you will receive a discount of 15% vs the actual listing price.
Presale will be available on https://sale.magia.finance/

What happens with the tokens I use to create a node?

When you create a node a portion of the tokens are swapped to AVAX to build up the treasury
to later be used to invest in high-yield DeFi protocols, here is the current distribution:
5% into ROLAND-AVAX LP
10% into Team Pool (marketing/development)
80% into Rewards Pool
Additional details are available in our documentation

What is the price of a node?

Nodes can be purchased from our dashboard, you need at least 10 $ROLAND tokens for each node

Can I sell my node(s)?

No, buying nodes is a one- time purchase and forever bound to the wallet address you used to create them.
Nodes will generate lifetime passive- income!

How many nodes can I buy?

There is currently a maximum of 100 nodes per wallet.

Smart-Contracts

All our smart contracts, balances and locked liquidity are publicly available, and verifiable.

Auditing

In-house audits and quality assurance process aside, the ROLAND team is always looking to get additional audits to ensure that the contracts are constantly being reviewed and are as secure as possible. We are looking forward to making our first public audit by the end of Q2 2022.

Bug Bounty Reward Program

To protect ROLAND's community and ensure their funds are safe, our smart contracts and web infrastructure are eligible to bug bounties up to $5,000 worth of MIM depending on the severity. All the reports must be strictly reported to redteam @ ROLAND.finance and be PGP-encrypted. Our public key is available here.

Anonymity / KYC

We have pushed considerable efforts in making each member of our team anonymous as well. Our reasons are simple:
We don't want fame but make a difference
We want ROLAND to be faceless, ultimately being entirely autonomous and decentralized
We want to protect the team from extortion and kidnapping
We will be fully transparent about any deciscions taken or funds transferred.
